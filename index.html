About the Film

A tired commuter waits on a late-night MBTA Orange Line platform at North Station. The delay sign never changes. As time passes, something feels wrong—the platform is too empty, too cold, the posters too old.

A janitor reveals the terrible truth: the train has been delayed since 1987. The commuter looks down to discover they're wearing a faded '86 Celtics championship shirt. They don't remember putting it on. They don't remember anything before the platform.

Frozen in time at the peak of Boston sports glory, eternally waiting for a train that will never come.
